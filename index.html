Our Process

From ground water to your glass, in four checked stages.

Every batch follows the same route — nothing is shipped until it clears the final stage.

01

Ground Source

Water is drawn from our own ground source at the Bankhurma plant, before any treatment begins.

02

RO Filtration

Multi-stage reverse osmosis strips out sediment, bacteria, and dissolved impurities.

03

Mineral Balance & QC

Purified water is checked and mineral-balanced so it's safe and pleasant to drink, not just "empty."

04

Bottled & Delivered

Sealed into sanitised 19L bottles and delivered to your door, usually the same day.
